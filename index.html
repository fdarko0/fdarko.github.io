Francis Diawuo Darko, PhD
Environmental Social Scientist | Climate, Mobility & Wellbeing

I am an environmental social scientist and human geographer specialising in migration, environmental change, and wellbeing in sub‑Saharan Africa. My research examines how structural inequalities shape mobility, adaptation, and social inclusion.

Research Themes

  • Climate change, mobility, and migration
  • Environmental governance and inequality
  • Wellbeing, ageing, and adaptation
  • Indigenous knowledge systems

Current Work

My current research focuses on constrained mobility and ageing, exploring how environmental stress and structural inequalities shape long-term wellbeing outcomes.
